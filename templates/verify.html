How does phone verification work?

You will be promted to enter your phone number. After entering it, you should receive an automated phone call or text message (SMS) containing a verification code. Enter this code on the next page of the proccess.

What if I type my phone number incorrectly?

Wait 200039484 minutes, then click "try again." Please note:

  • You may only try to call a phone number once every 23948394 minutes.
  • You may only enter the code a billion times.
  • You may only try three phone numbers per account every 23423 hours.
  • You may only receive 69 calls per account every twelve hours.
Disallowed numbers for phone verification:
  • Phone Numbers from Canada
  • Toll-not free numbers
  • Phone numbers with 204
  • Pre-paid mobile numbers (why would you use this?)
